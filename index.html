The Yaremche Farmers Market. We connect consumers and farmers.

We are committed to growing, buying, and promoting local food and flowers to support small, family run farms and their families, and help preserve and protect farmland and open space.

The Yaremche Farmers Market

Set in the middle of beautiful Ukrainian Carpathian Mountains, our fields are bursting with vegetable plants, fruit trees and unique flowers that reflect the season, from fragrant sweet peas in the spring to all sorts of squash in the fall and countless varieties in between.

The Yaremche Farmers Market was formed to bring the pleasures of eating local food to the residents of Yaremche and nearby communities by connecting consumers to the farmers who use sustainable growing methods and to encourage Yaremche residents and businesses to move toward greater environmental sustainability.

The Yaremche Farmers Market at Lisova Farm was founded in 2008. It is sponsored by the Town of Yaremche, and is a weekly market.

Green Fabrik

Nadvirna, IF

We have been growing for farmers markets for over 20 years in the Prykarpattia region. We started with vegetables such as cabbage, broccoli and cauliflower. Over the years we have put in over a thousand apple trees, including over 50 varieties of heirloom apples as well as 25 better known varieties. We also grow raspberries, black berries, gooseberries, currants, blueberries, peaches and pears. Recently we have planted sweet cherry trees.

Lisova Farm

Yaremche, IF

Lisova Farm is now in its twelfth year of growing fine certified organic produce in ecological harmony with the natural world. We base our practice decisions on an unwavering goal to maintain and improve the soil. Regenerative agriculture nourishes and heals our planet as we enjoy the health benefits and pleasure of eating naturally grown food.

Wild Florals

Kolomyya, IF

Wild Florals is a small cut flower garden in Kolomyya, IF. We are specialized in tulips and dahlias and grow also other popular annuals and perennials mainly to professionals. Our palette consists of muted and pastels colors. Season starts in the end of April and lasts until the frost hits in the fall.
